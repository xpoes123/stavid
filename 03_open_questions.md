# Open Questions

Things that came up in brainstorming but weren't resolved. Decide these before or during build.

---

## 1. What happens when you win a series?
- Is it just intrinsic satisfaction?
- Does the "winner" (more wins that week) get to pick Saturday's date activity?
- Is there a shared reward if you BOTH win your series?
- Currently no answer — may want to start without a reward and see if motivation holds

## 2. What's the consequence for losing a series?
- Leading idea: loser does something outside their social comfort zone
  - Has real teeth for Stephanie given social anxiety
  - Risk: fear-based motivation can cause avoidance of the whole system over time
  - Must feel like something they opted into, not imposed
- Alternative ideas not yet explored
- **Recommendation**: Decide this together before launching — the consequence needs buy-in from both or it won't work

## 3. How strict is the TikTok cap?
- Is 91 minutes a loss, or is there grace?
- Should the bot ask "did you stay under 90 min" (self-reported) or is there a way to pull screen time data?
- Self-reporting is fine to start — the system only works if you're honest with yourself

## 4. What counts as a "personal project" for David's Pillar 3?
- Listed examples: sports modeling, automation tools, blog posts, personal brand
- Does chess count? (hobby/skill improvement)
- Does sports modeling research count even if nothing ships?
- Suggested answer: yes to both — 30 min of focused engagement on something you're building or improving

## 5. North star / longer term goals
- Neither David nor Stephanie has a clear north star right now
- David is uncertain between startup path vs. senior engineer path
- This system is designed to work WITHOUT a north star — it's about weekly habits
- But Sunday check-ins could gradually surface what matters: "what do you want to focus on this week?" over many weeks reveals patterns
- Revisit in ~2 months: what are the wins you're most proud of? That points toward the north star

## 6. Pillar definitions may need to evolve
- These are v1 — expect to adjust after 2-3 weeks
- Pillars that are never missed might need to be raised
- Pillars that are always missed might need to be broken down further
- Sunday review is the right time to adjust

## 7. What does the bot do when someone goes silent?
- If Stephanie doesn't respond to the daily ping, is that a loss or a null?
- Recommendation: send a follow-up ping once, then log as a loss after a cutoff time (e.g. midnight)
- But this is a product decision

## 8. Shared vs. individual series
- Currently framed as two parallel individual series
- Could also track a "shared series" — did you BOTH win today?
- This adds a cooperative layer which might strengthen the "growing together" goal
- Worth considering as a feature, not just two separate trackers
