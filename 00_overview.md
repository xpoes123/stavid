# The Playoff Week Framework

## Core Concept
A week is a best-of-7 playoff series. You need 4 wins to advance. You can't be eliminated until you hit 4 losses. This means:
- A 1-3 week where you win the last 3 is still a series win
- Losing early in the week is NOT a reason to give up — this must be communicated clearly by the system
- The framing is about direction and resilience, not perfection

## Players
- **David** — software engineer at Ramp, wants to compound on long-term growth, currently lacks a clear north star but has concrete daily habits to build
- **Stephanie** — tends to doom scroll TikTok after work, wants to be more present and finish things, motivated by social accountability and mild anxiety about looking bad

## Shared Goals
- Become healthier and more active
- Grow together, not in diverging directions
- Reduce passive consumption (TikTok doom scrolling)
- Build the habit of finishing things

## Weekly Rhythm (existing anchors)
- **Tuesday/Thursday**: Stephanie has game nights with coworkers — these are NOT automatic losses, they count as doing something vs. passive scrolling
- **Wednesday**: Communal boxing class (David often misses due to commitments)
- **Saturday**: Date day — go out to eat + activity
- **Sunday**: Reset and morning lounge day — designated for weekly goal review
